About The Author

Hello, I'm Yash Srivastava.

Writing has always been more than a hobby for me—it is a way of bringing ideas, mysteries, and emotions to life. Every story begins with curiosity, and every page is an invitation to discover something new.

My debut novel, Tree Under Shed, marks the beginning of my journey as an author. Through suspense and imagination, I hope to create stories that remain in the minds of readers long after they finish the last page.
